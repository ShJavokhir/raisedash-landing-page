## A sharper focus

Raisedash now focuses on driver orientation and training records for trucking fleets. Safety teams can send training by SMS or email, drivers complete it in a phone browser, and the fleet tracks progress in one place.

## What that means

The platform is organized around getting new drivers ready before day one and preserving durable training evidence a fleet can produce on request. That direction shapes what we build next, from pre-arrival programs to structured first-90-days onboarding and a clean, exportable record of every version, score, and signature.

## New website and platform pages

We have refreshed the website and added platform pages that explain this focus in plain terms, so a safety director or owner can see what Raisedash does without a sales call.

## Academy and TruckTalk continue

Raisedash Academy and TruckTalk, our driver practice tools, continue as before. Nothing changes for drivers using them today. The shift is in where the core of Raisedash points: driver readiness and durable training evidence.

This is a direction update. We will keep sharing specific features in these notes as they ship.
