## Your Compliance Command Center

Before, getting the full picture of your compliance status meant clicking through multiple screens. Documents here, driver files there, violations somewhere else.

The new compliance dashboard brings it all together.

## What You'll See

**At the top:**
- Your overall DOT compliance score
- Document compliance percentage
- Driver qualification file status
- Active violations count

**In the charts:**
- Driver compliance breakdown (compliant vs. issues)
- Daily score trends over the past month

**Quick actions:**
- Jump to documents that need attention
- View drivers with compliance gaps
- Access inspection records
- Generate audit-ready reports
- Check FMCSA portal status
- Manage training programs
